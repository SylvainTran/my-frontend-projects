A Game Design Tool Motivation Thing

Project Pitch

This tool may be vaporware but it is ambitious. Loner game developers may use it to share their ideas to popular platforms like reddit, instagram and twitter while remaining hidden behind clouds. Doing so (and staying committed to daily or weekly posting agreement) will help them complete their passion projects in the world of video games.
